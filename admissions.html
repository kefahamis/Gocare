Admission Requirements
Accredited · Competency Based · Course Specific

Welcome to GoCare Training Institute. Our admissions process is designed to be simple, transparent, and student friendly, ensuring every applicant begins their journey with clarity and confidence.

General Requirements

Admission requirements are specific to each course and follow the standards set by regulatory and examination bodies (TVETA, TVET CDACC, NITA, KNEC, KHPOA).

Required Documents
  • KCPE / KCSE Certificate or Result Slip
  • National ID / Passport or Birth Certificate
  • 3 Passport size photos
  • Application fee
Step by step
Admission procedure
Follow these steps to complete your application to GoCare Training Institute.
1
Choose your course
Browse our programmes and select the course matching your goals and qualifications.
2
Submit your application
Complete the online form or visit any of our campuses with your documents.
3
Receive your offer letter
Our team reviews your application and sends an official offer letter and reporting date.
4
Pay your fees
Confirm your place by paying your deposit. Flexible instalment plans are available.
5
Begin your studies
Receive your orientation schedule and start your GoCare journey toward a global future.
