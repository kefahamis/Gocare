Frequently Asked Questions

Home FAQs
01

Is GoCare Training Institute registered and accredited?

Yes. GoCare Training Institute is a fully registered and accredited technical training college by the Ministry of Education, The National Industrial Training Authority (NITA), The Technical and Vocational Education and Training Authority (TVETA), The Technical and Vocational Education and Training Curriculum Development, Assessment and Certification Council (TVET CDACC), and The Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC).

02

Where are you located?

NAIROBI CITY CAMPUS: Nairobi CBD, GatKim Complex, 4th Floor, Temple Road.

THIKA ROAD / RUIRU CAMPUS: Eastern Bypass, Kamakis, Ruiru.

03

Do you provide attachment placement?

Yes. Through our Industry Liaison office, we guarantee clinical attachment placements for all our healthcare students in over 50 partner hospitals.
