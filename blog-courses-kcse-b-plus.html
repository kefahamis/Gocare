Best Courses for KCSE B+ (Plus)

Top marketable, practical, and career-focused programmes for KCSE B+ students — access to the full range of Diploma programmes.

6 min read 23 May 2026 Admissions Guide

A KCSE B+ (Plus) gives students access to the full range of advanced Diploma programmes. B+ learners can pursue professional training leading to respected, well-paying careers across healthcare, community development, social work, and business.

1. Diploma in Perioperative Theatre Technology – Level 6

Entry: KCSE C and above • TVET CDACC

High-demand surgical support. Learn: Theatre setup, Sterilisation, Surgical instrument handling, Infection prevention, Patient preparation. Work: Hospitals, Surgical centres, Theatre departments.

2. Diploma in Orthopaedic & Trauma Medicine – Level 6

Entry: KCSE C & Required Subjects • TVET CDACC

Learn: Trauma care, Orthopaedic support, Patient handling, Rehabilitation assistance. Work: Hospitals, Orthopaedic clinics, Trauma centres.

3. Diploma in Community Health – Level 6

Entry: KCSE C– and above • TVET CDACC

Learn: Community diagnosis, Health promotion, Disease prevention, Public health systems. Work: NGOs, County health departments, Public health projects.

4. Diploma in Mortuary Science – Level 6

Entry: KCSE C– and above • TVET CDACC

Learn: Human anatomy, Body preservation, Mortuary operations, Infection control. Work: Hospitals, Funeral homes, County morgues.

5. Diploma in Social Work – Level 6

Entry: KCSE C– and above • TVET CDACC

Learn: Social development, Counselling, Community engagement, Case management. Work: NGOs, Community programmes, Government agencies.

6. Certificate in Healthcare Support Services (CNA) – Level 5

Entry: KCSE D+ and above • TVET CDACC / KHPOA

Learn: Patient care, Vital signs, Infection control, Bedside assistance. Work: Hospitals, Clinics, Homecare agencies, International markets.

7. Certificate in Community Health – Level 5

Entry: KCSE D+ and above • TVET CDACC

Learn: Health promotion, Disease prevention, Health education, Field outreach. Work: NGOs, County health offices, Community programmes.

8. Caregiver – Level 4

Entry: KCSE Certificate • NITA

Learn: Basic patient care, Hygiene, Feeding and mobility, Home-based care. Work: Homes, Clinics, Elderly care facilities.
