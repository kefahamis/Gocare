Course Overview

The Certificate in Housekeeping – Level 3 is a foundational, competency-based program that equips learners with the essential skills required to deliver professional housekeeping services in hotels, resorts, corporate offices, institutions, and residential environments.

Learners gain hands-on competencies in guestroom cleaning, public area cleaning, laundry operations, hygiene and sanitation, equipment handling, linen management, and workplace safety. The course also develops essential soft skills such as communication, environmental responsibility, employability skills, and occupational safety practices.

This nationally recognized Level 3 qualification provides a strong entry point into the hospitality and accommodation industry and serves as a pathway to advanced programs in Housekeeping Supervision, Accommodation Operations, and Hospitality Management.

Industrial Attachment

All learners undertake a two (2) month attachment in:

  • Hotels & Resorts
  • Corporate Offices
  • Hospitality Establishments
  • Residential Facilities
  • Cleaning & Facilities Management Companies

GoCare provides full placement support to ensure every student gains real industry experience.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Certificate in Housekeeping – Level 3 can work as:

  • Room Attendant
  • Laundry Attendant
  • Public Area Cleaner
  • Housekeeping Assistant
  • Accommodation Support Staff
  • Professional Cleaner
  • Floor Supervisor
  • Housekeeping Manager

This qualification opens doors to diverse opportunities in hospitality, accommodation, and corporate service environments.
