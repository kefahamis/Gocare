Student Benefit

Second Course Sponsorship

At GoCare Training Institute, we are committed to maximizing your value. Through our Second Course Sponsorship program, eligible students can receive a second professional qualification at no extra tuition cost, giving you a dual-edge in the competitive job market.

Instant Activation
Dual Qualification

How Sponsorship Works

We believe that a multi-skilled professional is more employable. That's why we offer a unique sponsorship for a second course to students who enroll in our primary diploma and certificate programs.

Program Highlights

  • Enroll in a primary course (e.g., Nursing Assistant) and get a secondary certification (e.g., SDC Canada) free.
  • Only pay for examination and registration fees for the second course.
  • Expand your skill set without doubling your tuition investment.
  • Graduate with two recognized certificates or diplomas.

Eligible Programs

Sponsorship typically applies to short professional courses and international certifications that complement our main healthcare and hospitality programs. Check with the admissions office for the current list of sponsored pairings.
