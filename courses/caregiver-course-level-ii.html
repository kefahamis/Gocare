Course Overview

The Caregiver Course – Level II is a comprehensive, NITA-accredited program that prepares learners to become professional caregivers and patient attendants capable of delivering safe, compassionate, and high-quality support in hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, hospices, and home-based care environments.

Learners gain essential skills in patient hygiene, feeding, mobility assistance, repositioning, communication, and basic supportive nursing procedures. The course also trains students to observe and report patient concerns to nurses and other healthcare professionals, ensuring continuity of care and patient safety.

This approved Caregiver Level II program offers standardized, nationally recognized training and certification. Graduates qualify for multiple professional roles, including Patient Attendant, Patient Care Technician, Nursing Assistant, Health Care Assistant, and Professional Caregiver.
