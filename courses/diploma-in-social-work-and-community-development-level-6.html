2 Years Duration
Level 6 Level
Monthly Intakes

Course Overview

The Diploma in Social Work – Level 6 is an advanced, nationally recognized qualification designed to prepare learners to become competent, ethical, and community-focused social work professionals.

This program equips trainees with the knowledge, skills, and practical competencies required to support individuals, families, and communities through social interventions, empowerment programs, advocacy, crisis response, rehabilitation, and community development initiatives.

Learners are trained to assess social issues, design and implement community programs, provide psychosocial support, manage community-based groups, coordinate social services, conduct social research, and participate in policy implementation. The curriculum blends theory, practical skills, and extensive field exposure to ensure graduates are job-ready and aligned with Kenya’s social development priorities.

This Level 6 qualification provides a strong foundation for careers in NGOs, government agencies, community organizations, and international development institutions, and serves as a pathway to higher-level social sciences and community development programs.

Entry Requirements

Academic Qualification

KCSE Mean Grade C (Minus) or Level 5 Certificate in Social Work & Community Development.

Industrial Attachment

All learners undertake a Six (6) Month Industrial Field Attachment. GoCare provides full placement support to ensure every student gains real field experience.
