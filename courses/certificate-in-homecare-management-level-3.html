Programme Overview

The Health Care Assistant / Caregiver programme at GoCare Training Institute is designed to equip learners with the practical skills and theoretical knowledge required to provide high-quality care to individuals in various healthcare settings.

Our curriculum focuses on patient safety, personal hygiene, basic nursing care, and effective communication. Graduates are highly sought after by hospitals and international agencies due to our hands-on training approach in modern labs.
