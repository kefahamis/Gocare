Major Intakes

GoCare Training Institute offers three major intakes annually to ensure flexibility for our prospective students:

  • January Intake: Applications close on December 15th.
  • May Intake: Applications close on April 15th.
  • September Intake: Applications close on August 15th.

Orientation Dates

All new students are required to attend orientation. Exact dates will be communicated via your admission letter, but generally occur one week before classes commence.
