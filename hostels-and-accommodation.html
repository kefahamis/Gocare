Hostels & Accommodation

Safe, comfortable, and affordable student accommodation near our Nairobi campus — so you can focus entirely on your studies.

Apply Now Contact Us

Why Students Love GoCare Hostels

Discover why our students feel right at home in our modern and secure campus hostels.

Safe & Secure Environment

Your safety is our priority. Our hostels offer:

  • 24/7 security
  • Well-lit surroundings
  • Controlled access
  • A peaceful, student-friendly atmosphere

Parents trust GoCare because we provide a secure and protected living environment.

Modern, Clean & Comfortable Rooms

Our hostels are designed to support a healthy and productive student lifestyle, with:

  • Clean, well-maintained rooms
  • Comfortable beds
  • Adequate ventilation
  • Quiet study spaces
  • Reliable water supply

Students enjoy a comfortable home-away-from-home experience.

Convenient Location Near Campus

GoCare Training Institute hostels are located close to our campuses, making it easy for students to:

  • Attend classes on time
  • Access labs and study areas
  • Participate in group work
  • Save on transport costs

This convenience enhances academic performance and student wellbeing.

Supportive Student Community

Living in GoCare hostels allows students to:

  • Build friendships
  • Study together
  • Share experiences
  • Support each other
  • Grow socially and academically

Our hostels foster a positive, respectful, and inclusive community.

Affordable & Flexible Options

GoCare Training Institute offers accommodation at affordable rates, ensuring students from all backgrounds can access safe and comfortable housing.

Benefits include:

  • Reasonable pricing
  • Flexible payment options
  • Value for money

We believe quality accommodation should be accessible to all learners.

Who Our Hostels Are Ideal For

  • Students from outside Nairobi.
  • Learners who prefer a structured, safe environment.
  • Students who want to minimize transport costs.
  • Young learners who need close supervision.
  • Anyone seeking a peaceful, study-friendly space.

Whether you are a first-time student or continuing your studies, GoCare hostels provide the perfect environment for academic success.

A Home That Supports Your Success

At GoCare Training Institute, accommodation is more than a place to sleep — it is a support system that helps learners stay focused, motivated, and safe.

Your comfort matters. Your safety matters. Your success matters.
