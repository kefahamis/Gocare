Why Choose GoCare

Discover what sets GoCare Training Institute apart – world-class faculty, modern clinical labs, strong industry partnerships, and a proven track record of graduate success.

Apply Now Learn More
01

Fully Accredited & Regulator-Aligned

GoCare Training Institute is registered, licensed, and accredited by:

  • Ministry of Education
  • TVETA
  • TVET CDACC
  • NITA
  • KHPOA
  • KNEC

This ensures your qualification is valid, recognized, and trusted by employers and institutions locally and internationally.

02

International Certifications (Global Advantage)

GoCare is an approved global examination center for:

  • AMCA – USA
  • SDC Canada
  • ICDL Global – Ireland

Learners gain access to internationally recognized certifications that significantly enhance global employability.

03

Modern, Practical, Competency-Based Training

Our programs are designed to meet real industry needs, with:

  • Hands-on practical sessions
  • Modern labs and training facilities
  • Competency-based education (CBET)
  • Real-world simulations
  • Qualified, experienced trainers

You graduate job-ready, not just exam-ready.

04

Guaranteed Industrial Attachment

GoCare Training Institute has established partnerships with:

  • Hospitals
  • Hotels
  • NGOs
  • Corporates
  • Funeral homes
  • General Businesses
  • Government agencies
  • Private sector organizations

Every learner is guaranteed industrial attachment, ensuring practical exposure and real-world experience.

05

Two Strategic Campuses

GoCare offers accessible, well-equipped campuses:

  • Nairobi City Campus (within the CBD)
  • Thika Road / Ruiru Campus (with Hostels)

Both campuses provide:

  • Modern classrooms
  • Practical labs
  • Student support services
  • Safe learning environments
06

Flexible Study Modes

We understand that learners have different schedules. That’s why we offer:

  • Daytime classes
  • Evening classes
  • Saturday classes
  • Online / Distance learning

This flexibility allows students to learn while working or managing other responsibilities.

07

Affordable Fees & Flexible Payment Plans

GoCare is committed to making quality education accessible. We offer:

  • Affordable tuition fees
  • Flexible installment plans
  • MPESA and bank payment options
  • Second International Course Sponsorship for selected programs

You get maximum value at an affordable cost.

08

Second Course Sponsorship (Free International Certification)

Eligible learners receive:

  • Additional internationally aligned training
  • Free SDC Canada certification
  • A second professional qualification
  • Enhanced employability locally and abroad

This is one of the best value-for-money offers in Kenya’s TVET sector.

09

High Graduate Employability

GoCare graduates are highly sought after because they are:

  • Competent
  • Practical
  • Professionally trained
  • Internationally certified
  • Industry-ready

Our alumni work in hospitals, hotels, NGOs, corporates, government agencies, and international caregiving and healthcare roles.

10

Safe, Modern, Student-Friendly Environment

GoCare Training Institute provides:

  • Safe and secure hostels
  • Modern learning spaces
  • Supportive staff
  • A respectful, inclusive culture
  • A positive learning atmosphere

Students feel supported, valued, and empowered.

11

Clear Progression Pathways

Learners can progress from:

  • Artisan → Certificate → Diploma → Higher Diploma → Degree

Your education does not stop at one level — GoCare Training Institute supports your long-term academic and career growth.

12

A Brand You Can Trust

Since 2018, GoCare Training Institute has built a strong reputation for:

  • Quality
  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Student success
  • Industry relevance

Parents trust us. Employers trust us. Students succeed with us.

Guaranteed Internships & Global Career Paths

We pride ourselves on our highly successful industrial and clinical placement programs. GoCare partners directly with leading national hospitals, healthcare networks, corporate companies, and international agencies. We ensure that every student secures a valuable hands-on attachment to successfully bridge the gap between academic training and active career employment.

GoCare Is Where Your Future Begins

Whether you want to build a career in healthcare, hospitality, social sciences, or business, GoCare Training Institute provides the training, support, and global opportunities you need to succeed.

Your journey to a brighter future starts here!
