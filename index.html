  About Us

Kenya's Most Trusted
Healthcare Institute

GoCare Training Institute is a fully registered, government-accredited college dedicated to delivering world-class healthcare education. Since 2011, we have equipped thousands of students with the skills, knowledge and confidence to thrive in Kenya's growing healthcare sector.

Approved by TVET CDACC, NITA, AMCA, KNEC, KHPOA and international bodies ? our certificates and diplomas are recognised across Kenya and internationally. With two modern campuses in Nairobi and Ruiru, quality training is always within reach.

Why Choose GoCare Training Institute

Choosing the right training institution is one of the most important decisions a learner will ever make. At GoCare, we combine national accreditation, international certification, and industry-aligned training to deliver an exceptional learning experience.

GoCare is more than a college. It is a launchpad for your future.

1. Fully Accredited & Regulator Aligned

GoCare is registered, licensed, and accredited by major bodies including:

  • Ministry of Education
  • TVETA & TVET CDACC
  • NITA, KHPOA & KNEC

2. International Certifications

Approved global examination center for leading international bodies:

  • AMCA – USA
  • SDC Canada
  • ICDL Global – Ireland

3. Modern & Practical Training

Competency-Based Education (CBET) designed to meet real industry needs:

  • Hands-on practical sessions
  • Modern labs & facilities
  • Real-world simulations

4. Guaranteed Industrial Attachment

Strong partnerships with various sectors to ensure every learner gets exposure:

  • Hospitals & Hotels
  • NGOs & Corporates
  • Government Agencies

5. Two Strategic Campuses

Accessible and well-equipped learning environments:

  • Nairobi City Campus (CBD)
  • Ruiru Campus (with Hostels)

6. Flexible Study Modes

We offer various schedules to suit your busy lifestyle:

  • Day, Evening & Saturday classes
  • Online & Distance learning

7. Affordable & Flexible Fees

Quality education made accessible through various financial plans:

  • Affordable tuition fees
  • Flexible installment plans

8. Second Course Sponsorship

Get a second professional qualification for free in selected programs:

  • Free SDC Canada certification
  • Enhanced global employability

9. High Graduate Employability

Our graduates are highly sought after by top employers globally:

  • Healthcare & Hospitality
  • NGOs & Private sector

10. Student Friendly Environment

A positive learning atmosphere with full support services:

  • Safe & secure hostels
  • Supportive staff & culture

11. Clear Progression Pathways

Support for your long-term academic and career growth:

  • Artisan to Diploma and beyond

12. A Brand You Can Trust

Building a strong reputation for excellence since 2018:

  • Quality & Integrity
  • Proven student success
